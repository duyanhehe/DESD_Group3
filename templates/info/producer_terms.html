Producer Terms.

Last updated: May 2026

1. Eligibility

To sell on Food Network, producers must be legally authorized to sell food products in their jurisdiction. This includes maintaining all necessary health permits, licenses, and insurance.

2. Product Standards

We prioritize producers who follow organic, sustainable, or regenerative farming practices. All products must be accurately described, including any relevant certifications or growing methods.

3. Fees and Payments

Food Network charges a small commission on each sale to maintain the platform and support our mission. Payments are processed through our secure payment gateway and settled according to our settlement schedule.

4. Fulfillment

Producers are responsible for fulfilling orders within the specified timeframe and ensuring that products are packaged safely for transport.

Need clarification?

If you have questions about these terms, please contact our producer support team at producers@foodnetwork.market
